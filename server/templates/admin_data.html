Psychometric Curves by σ_target — All Participants Overlaid

Each panel shows one (context, σ_target) condition. Every participant's fitted Gaussian psychometric curve is plotted in a distinct colour, with their empirical response proportions as dots.

How to read this: if the noise calibration succeeded, curves within each panel should collapse onto one — all participants experience the same effective noise level σ_target, so the task is equally difficult for everyone regardless of their individual σ_internal.
